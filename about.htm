* About Us

 

Our company was established in February 1998 for the purpose of developing game software and has been developing game development engines and multi-functional integrated graphic tools.
In July 1998, we completed the development of our own Internet-based NETWORK engine and developed Dispel, an authentic role-playing game that supports FREE BATTLE NET.

Furthermore, we are currently developing "Pranks of Gods" online in 2000 and plan to commercially service "Dispel" and "Pranks of Gods" online on the Internet using our self-developed NETWORK engine.
At our ABALON STUDIO development lab, we are working to get closer to our customers to provide better quality game services 24 hours a day.
